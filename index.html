Character Biography:

  • Full Name: Emilie de Rochefort (Also Known As Lili!)
  • Age: 18
  • Nationality: Monaco
  • First Appearance: Tekken 5
  • Lili is a skilled gymnast and fighter. Her love for fighting began when Lili was kidnapped and held ransom. From there, she broke free which sparked her love for fighting!

Fighting Abilities

  • Increased Agility
  • Gymnastics Background
  • Acrobatic Moveset
  • Immense Flexibility
  • In the game, Lili is known for having the best 'Side-Step' which always trips up players allowing her to avoid attacks effectively!

Enemies and Friends!

  • Bestest Friend Forever: Asuka Kazama: Whether players of Tekken consider the two to be on the more frienemie side of things, it is no doubt that these two share a special connection. Much of Lilis stoy has surrounded around asuka and has made the two one of the most iconic duos in the franchise!
  • Family Friend <33: Sebastian: Since her childhood, Sebastian has taken close care and has supported her love for fighting. Through her entire time in the Tekken franchise, he has always been by her side.
  • Main Enemy: Kazuya Mishima: Although Lili's love for fighting started when she was kidnapped, the Mishima Corporation has only further ignited the fire inside of her to fight. A global war broke out due to the actions of the Corporation causing Lili's father to full under Medical stress. Since then, Lili has been devoted to fighting for her father!

Appearance of Lili

  • Lili's character designed is easily recognizable due to her long blonde hair and her signature hime cut. She is usually seen wearing a frilly white dress matched with knee high white boots. Her style is very elegant to match her wealthy background. She as well has other outfits that are more inspired by urban fashion and streetwear showing duality in her personality!
  • Lili is also the tallest tekken character reaching 5'11!

    • Overall, Lili is a vary versatile fighter. Her personality, combined with her unique fighting style has made her a fan favorite amongst millions of players!
